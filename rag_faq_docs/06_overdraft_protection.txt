Overdraft Protection
Overdraft protection prevents declined transactions when your balance drops below zero up to a pre-approved limit. Enrollment is free. Overdraft amounts accrue interest at 21 percent per annum calculated daily.
A $5 fee is charged each time the account enters overdraft within a statement period. An NSF fee of $45 applies if you exceed your overdraft limit. Limits range from $500 to $5,000 depending on your credit profile.
