Applying for a Personal Loan
Loans from $2,000 to $50,000 with terms of 1 to 7 years at fixed rates of 6.99 to 19.99 percent. Apply online, by phone, or at a branch.
Provide proof of income, photo ID, and your SIN. Decisions on amounts up to $15,000 are typically instant. Early repayment is permitted without penalty.
