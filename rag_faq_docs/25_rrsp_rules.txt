RRSP Contribution Rules
The 2024 annual limit is 18 percent of previous year earned income up to $31,560. The 2024 contribution deadline is March 3, 2025.
Unused room carries forward indefinitely. Over-contributions attract a 1 percent per month penalty. RRSP accounts must be converted to a RRIF or annuity by December 31 of the year you turn 71.
