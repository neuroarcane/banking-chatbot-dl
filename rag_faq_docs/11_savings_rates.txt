Savings Account Interest Rates
Standard savings earns 1.75 percent per annum compounded monthly. High-interest savings earns 3.50 percent up to $500,000. Interest is calculated on the daily closing balance and credited on the last business day of each month.
TFSA accounts earn the high-interest rate. RRSP accounts earn 3.75 percent. GIC rates: 1-year 4.85 percent, 2-year 4.90 percent, 5-year 5.10 percent.
