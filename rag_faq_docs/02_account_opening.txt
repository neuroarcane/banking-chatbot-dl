Requirements to Open a Bank Account
You need two pieces of government-issued identification. Primary ID includes a passport, driver licence, or provincial photo ID. Secondary ID can be a credit card, social insurance card, or utility bill dated within 90 days.
Applicants must be at least 18 years old, or 16 with a co-signer. Non-residents may open accounts with a valid work or study permit. Accounts can be opened in person at any branch or online through our digital banking portal.
