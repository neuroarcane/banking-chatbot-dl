Student Banking Account
Available to full-time students aged 16 to 25. No monthly fee, no minimum balance, and unlimited debit transactions and e-Transfers. Bring student ID, government photo ID, and proof of enrollment to apply.
The account converts to standard chequing at age 25 or graduation. Includes a free first box of 50 cheques and a no-fee student credit card with a $500 limit subject to credit approval.
