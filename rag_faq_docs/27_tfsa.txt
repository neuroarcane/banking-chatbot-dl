TFSA Overview
Contributions grow tax-free and withdrawals are tax-free at any time. The 2024 annual contribution room is $7,000. Unused room accumulates since 2009 or age 18.
Over-contributions attract a 1 percent per month penalty. Withdrawn amounts can be re-contributed the following calendar year. TFSAs can hold cash, GICs, mutual funds, ETFs, and stocks.
