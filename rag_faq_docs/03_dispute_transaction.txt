How to Dispute a Transaction
File a dispute within 60 days of the statement date. Log in to online banking, navigate to the transaction, and select Dispute this transaction. Provide details about why the charge is incorrect or unauthorized.
Investigations take 5 to 10 business days. A temporary credit may be applied while the review is underway. If resolved in your favour the credit becomes permanent. If not, the amount is reversed and you are notified in writing.
